Need-Centric Curriculum

The general business landscape is contextually dynamic, complex and riddled with economic uncertainties. As no two challenges are absolutely the same, we develop our programs taking into consideration the needs of our clients vis-a-vis the contextual underpinnings of the environment. By so doing, we ensure that active learning that aids productivity and positive behavioral change takes place.

Faculty

Our programs are led by full-time professionals and globally-trained alumni of some of the most prestigious world-class institutions of learning. Our faculty are chosen for their expertise as well as their proven ability to also teach senior executives. Many are scholar-practitioners actively engaged in shaping policy through consulting and advisory work with heads of organizations and governments.

Participants

Strong synergy is created in our programs due to our participants mix. This is because, we create a learning atmosphere where diverse individuals with different learning styles and experiences across ranges of industries, job functions and levels; interact together.

Their perspectives are aligned to the cutting-edge research and practices that permeates their individuality and creates professional bond that aids relational trust. They share ideas and insights necessary for improved personal and organizational performance.

Our Training Model

Our programs emphasize practical solutions for participants. It is designed to challenge held assumptions, disrupt ways of doing things, and introduce participants to unexpected ways of thinking and mental modeling. We make extensive use of the case study method where applicable, enabling participants to explore new analytical frameworks and the latest research within the context of real-world business decision making. Participants build skills through group interactions in case discussions, simulations, and team exercises. They leave the programs with the willingness to apply what they learn directly to their current challenges. Our models ensure that learning is accelerated by sharing winning strategies and our participants usually return to their organizations ready to lead change and make the most of every opportunity for effectiveness and growth.

Our Mentoring Model

We ensure a continuous follow-through by apportioning reasonable time in mentoring participants long after the in-person session.
